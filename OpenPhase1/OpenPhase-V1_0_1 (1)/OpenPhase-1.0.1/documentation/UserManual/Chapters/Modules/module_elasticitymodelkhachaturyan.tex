The \cvar{EffectiveElasticConstants} are calculated by calling the method \cmethod{SetEffectiveElasticConstants(EP)} as
\begin{equation}
  \CB = \sum_\alpha \CB_\alpha  \phi_\alpha
\end{equation}
The \cvar{EffectiveEigenStrains} are calculated by calling the method \cmethod{SetEffectiveEigenStrains(EP)}
\begin{equation}
  \varepsilon^* = \sum_\alpha \phi_\alpha \varepsilonB^*_\alpha
\end{equation}
The driving force $\Delta G^{el}_{\alpha\beta}$ becomes
\begin{align}
  \Delta G^{el}_{\alpha\beta} &= \frac{1}{2} \varepsilonB^{el} \left(\CB_\beta-\CB_\alpha\right)\varepsilonB^{el} + \varepsilon^{el} \CB \left(\varepsilonB^*_\beta-\varepsilonB^*_\alpha\right)\\
  &= \frac{1}{2} \varepsilonB^{el} \left(\CB_\beta-\CB_\alpha\right)\varepsilonB^{el} - \sigmaB \left(\varepsilonB^*_\beta-\varepsilonB^*_\alpha\right)
\end{align}
